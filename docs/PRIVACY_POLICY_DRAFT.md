# Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 2026-06-30

Renegade Millionaire is operated by Brass Balls Marketing. This draft is prepared for web and app store review and should be reviewed before public launch.

## Data We Collect

- Account details: email address, name, authentication identifiers, and session metadata.
- Course progress: completed modules, section responses, worksheet entries, and timestamps.
- Goals and planning data: WARRIOR category goals, deadlines, measurements, action lists, progress, and archived goal state.
- Battle Report data: daily priorities, habits, streak notes, energy, focus, water, weight, reflections, weekly reset notes, and Warrior Score entries.
- Profile practice data: routines, affirmations, creed lines, and reminder preferences when enabled.
- Technical data: device, browser, app version, logs, and diagnostics needed to run and secure the service.

## How We Use Data

We use this data to provide the course app, save progress, personalize the dashboard, protect accounts, troubleshoot errors, and improve the product.

## Health And Wellness Note

The app includes habit, routine, weight, energy, focus, and self-improvement tracking. This information is user-entered for personal planning and is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by service providers used to run the app, including hosting, authentication, database, analytics, support, and email providers.

## Security

User data is stored in Supabase with row-level security policies designed to restrict user-owned records to the authenticated account owner. No system can guarantee absolute security, but access controls, authentication, and transport security are used to protect the service.

## Data Retention

We retain account and app data while the account is active or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

## User Choices

Users may request account deletion, correction, export, or support by contacting Brass Balls Marketing. App store builds should include the final support email and account deletion workflow before public release.

## Children

The app is not intended for children under 13.
